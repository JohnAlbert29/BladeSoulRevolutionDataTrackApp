Mythic Pet Evolution Calculator

Evolution Rules:

  • Mythic Pets can be evolved to Mythic [True] Pets, and Mythic [True] Pets can be further evolved to Mythic [Ultimate] Pets.
  • When using the same Pet or higher evolution-tier Pet as material, the success rate is 100%.
  • Using a different Pet of the same grade gives 66% success rate.
  • Upon failure: Mythic [True] evolution grants 50 'Super Pet Aura', Mythic [Ultimate] grants 100 'Super Pet Aura'.
